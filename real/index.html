i didn’t build this site to impress anyone.
there’s no javascript wizardry here.
no animations.
no fancy scroll effects.

just HTML.
like my thoughts raw, unstyled, borderline broken.

because sometimes, plain is the most honest way to be seen.

i joined web3 thinking it was liberation.
“own your data,” they said.
“be the bank,” they said.
now i’m refreshing Discord, tracking wallets, and roleplaying as a community manager for a DAO that hasn’t paid anyone in months.

my wallet? empty.
my calendar? full.
my soul? wrapped in ERC-20.

people say “code is law.”
but here, law is whoever has the most followers or the fastest rug.

i’ve written more in Notion docs than in love letters.
i’ve been ghosted by projects harder than by my ex.
i’ve deployed things i don’t understand, prayed they work, and called it innovation.

this website is simple because i’m tired.
and because not everything needs to be “built different” to be real.

sometimes a webpage,
just like a person,
is enough as-is.
even if it’s just
HTML.

hire me before i get hired by your competitor.
